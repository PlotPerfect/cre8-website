Our LED sign-lighting modules are professional-grade solutions ideal for backlighting signage, particularly channel letters.
The flexible wiring between each module allows them to be positioned precisely to match the shape of the signage — perfect for intricate shapes such as letters or logos.

Our LED modules feature strong light output with a wide beam angle, offering an energy-efficient and visually striking backlighting solution.
They are IP67 rated, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor use.

We offer a wide range of LED modules in various styles and colours.
Options include single and double modules in solid colours, with a selection of colour temperatures such as bright warm white and cool white.
Also available are RGB LED modules, capable of producing over 16 million colours for dynamic and eye-catching effects.

Our Rigid Light Box Bars are available in 6500K and serve as an efficient replacement for traditional fluorescent light tubes.

We also provide the necessary copper wiring and compatible drivers (transformers) to support your LED setup and ensure optimal performance.

Adhesives / Accessories

Product Types

  • MITRE ADHESIVE: A fast-acting two-part bonding solution ideal for quick fixes and seamless joints in sign fabrication. Perfect for bonding trims, profiles, and panel edges with precision.
